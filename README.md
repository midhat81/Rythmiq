# 🎯 RYTHMIQ
### AI-Powered Personal Life Assistant

[![Live Demo](https://img.shields.io/badge/Live%20Demo-Click%20Here-2d8eff?style=for-the-badge&logo=vercel)](https://rythmiq-tau.vercel.app)
[![GitHub](https://img.shields.io/badge/GitHub-midhat81-black?style=for-the-badge&logo=github)](https://github.com/midhat81/Rythmiq)
[![React Native](https://img.shields.io/badge/React%20Native-Expo-blue?style=for-the-badge&logo=react)](https://expo.dev)
[![TypeScript](https://img.shields.io/badge/TypeScript-5.0-blue?style=for-the-badge&logo=typescript)](https://typescriptlang.org)

Rythmiq silently analyzes your weather, sleep, and energy then tells you exactly what to do, when to do it, and how to feel your best. No typing required.

🚀 **Live Demo:** https://rythmiq-tau.vercel.app

---

## ✨ Features

- ⛅ **Weather Intelligence** — Real-time weather analysis
- 🧠 **AI Decision Engine** — Proactive AI tells you what to do
- 🔋 **Energy Tracker** — Daily energy budget based on sleep and weather
- 🧬 **Weather DNA** — AI learns your unique weather preferences
- 🥾 **Hiking Guide** — Trail recommendations based on conditions
- 📅 **Daily Planner** — Smart morning and evening plans
- 🔔 **Smart Alerts** — Proactive weather and energy notifications
- 👤 **Profile and Stats** — Progress rings and achievements

## ⚙️ Installation

```bash
git clone https://github.com/midhat81/Rythmiq.git
cd Rythmiq/RythmiqApp
npm install
npm run web
```

---

## 🧠 How It Works

1. AI collects weather, sleep, and energy data silently
2. Decision engine analyzes everything together
3. Personalized advice is ready when you open the app
4. No typing. No asking. Just smart guidance.

---

## 🌟 Unique Features

- Proactive AI tells you things before you ask
- Energy Budget System like a battery for your body
- Micro-Window Alerts for exact perfect moments
- Weather DNA your unique personal weather profile
- Shadow Mode AI tracks what you actually did vs advice
